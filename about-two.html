Overview

Metercom group was incorporated on the 5th Jan 2024 as a private limited company. Our company is a dynamic telecommunications and marketing solutions provider. We specialize in delivering top-notch telecom services, including voice and data solutions, telecom Infrastructures' solutions, cloud-based services, and network management.
